    04 Jun 2024, 00:00

    A TIKTOKER has revealed her list of six must-haves at HomeGoods - but she suggested steering clear of two items.

    The TikTok video was keen on HomeGoods summer items but said shoppers should avoid some grocery items.

    "HomeGoods has some really beautiful stuff out for summer right now," TikToker Maddi (@peachyymaddi) said in the video.

    Maddi walked viewers through the store and pointed out troves of finds for homeowners.

    Here are Maddi's HomeGoods suggestions to buy and avoid.

    1. SUMMER DECORATIONS

    Maddi said she was keen on summer home decorations.

    Read More on HomeGoods

    "I love all the stuff that they have out with butterflies and flowers," she said.

    Maddi pointed out a replica of Michelangelo's famous David sculpture for $29.99 and a small garden gnome.

    Commenters agreed with Maddi's finds.

    One said: "That gnome with the pink flowers on its hat," with a series of heart emojis.

    Most read in Money

    2. MUGS AND CUPS

    Maddi was a fan of HomeGoods' wide variety of beverage holders.

    One of the first products she holds up as a suggestion is a four-pack of flowery plastic cups.

    She also finds multiple cute mugs in the store - a few are only $3.99.

    Some spark joy, including ones that say "Hello, Summer" and "Sunshine."

    Maddi was not a fan of one of the designer's cups.

    "You just have to sort through the Rae Dunn," she said, criticizing the designer well-known for placing silly sayings in thin-lined print on mugs.

    "There are so many other options to choose from that are elegant, cute, boogie."

    3. KITCHEN GOODS

    Maddi points out multiple kitchen necessities available in the shop.

    First, she pulls out a sunflower-themed salt and pepper shaker set.

    She has another suggestion later in the video.

    "HomeGoods is a great place to get pots and pans," she suggests.

    "As well as some cute and colorful kettles."

    The kitchen finds offer various colors - from light pinks to navy blues.

    4. MASON JAR SOAP DISPENSER

    Maddi said that she always loves the HomeGoods soap dispensers.

    "These Mason jar soap dispensers are always a steal for $3.99," she said.

    The jar has the famous canning jar logos on the side - HomeGoods retrofits the top of the jar with a silver push soap dispenser.

    5. COFFEE SYRUPS

    Maddi said the Torani coffee syrups are a staple of her HomeGoods shopping trips.

    "I always get these here," she said.

    Maddi's video captures several flavors on the shelves - white chocolate, chocolate macadamia, salted caramel, and Irish cream.

    6. JAM

    Maddi was skeptical of some of the HomeGoods grocery offerings - but she was a bigger fan of the jams.

    "I feel like this is worth the money," she said while pulling out a Stonewall Kitchen brand jam.

    The $5.99 jam is wild Maine blueberry vanilla flavor.

    A commenter on the video didn't agree with the product suggestion.

    They said: "I've had that specific jam... it's mid."

    MADDI'S CAUTIONARY ADVICE

    The advisor wasn't a fan of all of the HomeGoods offerings.

    Maddi asks: "In the food aisle, what are we doing?"

    She picks up a box of macaroons and meringues - both snack options appear crushed and smushed.

    "This goes to show - across Ross and Marshalls - don't get the macaroons."

    She also suggested - partially out of horror - to avoid buying calendars.

    "This is so concerning," she said.

    "Y'all, please don't let her get to 2024!"
