    WATERPROOF boots are the most reliable footwear around, ensuring that you're warm through years of hikes, muddy walks and wet weather - keeping dry never goes out of fashion.

    Popular durable boot brands include Hunter, Timbaland and UGG, although there are many other options of long-lasting kicks crafted specifically for extreme weather conditions!

    If you're getting into hiking or simply adore woodland walks, then you're going to need some boots with cushioned, gripped soles and tight fastenings to keep the water out if it rains.

    Don't want to ruin your trainers in wet weather? That's another great reason to buy some waterproof shoes! Thanks to utility-wear fashion trends, clunky boots look effortlessly cool.

    Whatever the reason you want to make this useful investment, there are so many styles available from wellies to waterproof Chelsea boots.

    To save you some time, we've found the most sturdy waterproof boots to buy now - did someone say booty call?
