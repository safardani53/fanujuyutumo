    Amazing dishwasher tablet hack can unblock your drains in seconds - here's how

    A SUPER-KEEN cleaning fan has revealed her trick for unclogging pesky drains in just moments with little more than a single - and cheap - dishwasher tablet. Tens of thousands of TikTok fans have given the thumbs-up to the simple measure explained by the UK-based @cleaningwithdanielled account.

    08 Mar 2024, 00:00

    A SUPER-KEEN cleaning fan has revealed her trick for unclogging pesky drains in just moments with little more than a single - and cheap - dishwasher tablet.

    Tens of thousands of TikTok fans have given the thumbs-up to the simple measure explained by the UK-based @cleaningwithdanielled account.

    A caption on her 25-second online clip promises: "Clogged drain? Dishwasher tablet will unblock your drains and it will eliminate any odours!"

    The footage shows a small dishwasher tablet being unwrapped from its plastic packaging, along with the instruction: "Use a dishwasher tablet with part boiled water."

    After placing the tablet within the plughole, warm water is poured on from a kettle.

    A plug is then restored on top of the now-disintegrated tablet before more part-boiled water is swished around for good measure.

    Danielle's video left many convinced the "hack" could leave both a sink gleaming and the drains free from any irritating blockages - without the need to call in a pricey plumber.

    Danielle's accompanying videos demonstrate her love of cleaning and tidying around the home, through a TikTok channel she has dubbed "CleanTok".

    Other video tips include giving thorough cleans to washing machines, and leaving scented Lenor wipes secreted around the home - including zipped inside sofa cushions and drawers of fresh laundry.

    Her dishwasher tablet clip has now been "liked" more than 21,700 times.

    Among the impressed comments below are "Your sink is so tiny and cute", "It works!", "Game changer", "I had to follow after this one wow" and "I like this hack".

    Danielle's is not the only dishwasher-related trick to impress online.

    Fellow TikTokker Billy Sarris told of ridding his washing machine of a "funky" smell by adding two dishwasher tablets to the drum while running it on the hottest setting.

    Other tips which have been offered on the video-sharing site include putting your dirty cloths and sponges in a large pot, along with a dishwasher tablet, and letting the items boil for about 15 minutes.

    Air fryers can also be given a much-needed freshening-up by judicious use of dishwasher tablets - while another man explained how he got crockery cleaner than ever depending on where you insert the tablet.
