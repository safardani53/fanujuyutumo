    Lia has been active in YouTube since December 2007. She created her first YouTube account on 17th of December 2007. During that time, this account was run by her mother as she used to post several videos in this YouTube account. From 2010, Lia began posting videos related to personal commentary, sketch comedy, and musical performances. She has also been active as an actress since 2008. In 2008, she appeared in No Man's Land and When Weather Changed History. In 2009, she appeared in the short movies like Shattered Allegiance, I Do and I Don't and Collide.
