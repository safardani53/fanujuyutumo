    Laura Harrier: Early Life & Education

    Born to biracial parents in Chicago who are African-American, Polish, and English, Laura is a native American. The actress was born on March 28, 1990, to parents Temujin Harrier and Linda Harrier.

    Her father works in an insurance company, and her mother is a speech pathologist. She has a brother named William Harrier. In her family, she is the first one to enter the showbiz industry.

    Laura had a speech impediment (defect in person’s speech, such as lisp or stammer) till the age of three, and her mother used to help in her speech. Also, to boost Laura’s confidence, her mom Linda put her in the acting classes.

    At the age of 17, Harrier moved to New York City to study set history but later decided to switch professions in modeling.

    Regarding her education, Laura went to Evanston High School in 2004 and graduated from there in 2008. Later Harrier enrolled at New York University but dropped out due to more time on modeling.

    After some time, she tried to take a chance on acting also. Thus, to sharpen her acting skills, Laura attended William Esper Studio High School. There she attended drama school for two years and then began working from there.
