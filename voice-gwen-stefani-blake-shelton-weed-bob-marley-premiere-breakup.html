    13 Feb 2024, 00:00

    BLAKE Shelton hinted that he smokes weed with girlfriend Gwen Stefani during Monday night’s season 19 premiere of The Voice. 

    The country star let it slip that the couple indulges in a lot of “Bob Marley products” as they continue to face rumors of a breakup after “difficult” months in quarantine. 

    The first episode of the latest season showed a virtual audience joining judges Blake and Gwen along with John Legend and Kelly Clarkson - who all appeared in-person at the studio. 

    One of the first contestants to take the stage was Hawaii native, Joseph Soul - who made John and Kelly hit their button with his rendition of Bob Marley’s Is This Love?

    Gwen - who famously identifies as a ska artist which originated off Marley’s home island of Jamaica - notably did not turn around for the tattooed contestant. 

    “It was really creative, I’ve never heard a version of the song like that,” Gwen said still impressed. 

    Blake, 44, looked shocked and chimed in: “That’s a Bob Marley song! 

    “I thought for sure you would’ve turned around for a Bob Marley song… we use ALL of his products!”

    Kelly, 38, began to crack up at the candid confession as the No Doubt front woman appeared mortified. 

    “You’re so stupid,” she yelled to her boyfriend.

    Meanwhile, John, 41, seemed to know what the country crooner was referring to with a big smirk on his face. 

    “And what products would those be, Blake?” the EGOT winner asked to tease his co-star. 

    The late legendary Three Little Birds singer was vocal about frequently using marijuana and was often pictured smoking before his death in 1981. 

    He even has a cannabis line, named Marley Natural, that the official Bob Marley brand and is sold in stores today. 

    The brand includes THC-infused flower, accessories, and body care products - which Blake implied he uses with his girlfriend.

    Fans took notice, and called the God’s Country singer out on Twitter: “Not blake talking about weed.”

    “I’m sure Blake Shelton just admitted he smoke weed on the voice,” another person wrote.

    One viewer joked: “Gwen is going to curse Blake's a** out back stage. ‘don’t be telling them I smoke weed mf, let me tell them.’”
