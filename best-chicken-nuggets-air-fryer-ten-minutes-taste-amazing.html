    THERE’S nothing better than chicken nuggets when you fancy eating something a bit cheeky - right?

    If you’re a fan of chicken nuggets but are fed up with the bog-standard ones you buy from the freezer aisles and want to know how to make your own, you’ve come to the right place.

    Food fan Jazz Leah regularly shares her quick and easy recipes to TikTok and one of her most recent ones shares her simple recipe for making chicken nuggets from scratch, using an air fryer.

    Jazz has amassed an impressive 121.7k followers and 1 million likes on the video sharing platform and has left people very hungry with her healthy chicken nugget recipe. 

    It’s safe to say that our mouths are now watering, so if you have joined the air fryer gang, this is one you’ll want to try.

    Jazz explained her simple chicken nugget recipe and said: “If you’re looking for a quick, week-night dinner, try these healthy air fried chicken nuggets, with a sriracha agave dipping sauce.
