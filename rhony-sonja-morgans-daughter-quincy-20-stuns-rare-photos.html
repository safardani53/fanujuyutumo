    REAL Housewives of New York City star Sonja Morgan's 20-year-old daughter Quincy Morgan STUNNED in a series of rare photos.

    In a set of new Instagram photos, the college student rocked a bikini and kissed her boyfriend.

    Last week, the RHONY star's daughter took to Instagram to share snaps of her daily life.

    While the first photo caught her lounging by the pool in a black bikini, the second showed her outside as the wind blew her hair and short skirt up.

    Other snaps showed her sitting on patio furniture on a balcony, enjoying a drink on the beach and her posing for a photo while out to dinner.

    One photo gave insight into her relationship with boyfriend JR as she balanced her phone on his head, while another photo showed her boyfriend standing with a face mask on.

    The slides continued with snaps of Quincy at the beach, while the final photo caught her placing her mouth on her boyfriend's ears.

    She simply captioned the set of photos: "TMI."

    Back in January, Quincy shared more candid snaps that gave Bravo fans a look into her day-to-day life.

    The first photo in the post showed her and JR embracing as they kissed, while following snaps included her on a swing, her posing with friends, a snap of her enjoying a sandwich and colorful artwork on a fence that read "top of the world."

    Other photos showed her boyfriend walking a dog, the young couple posing for a mirror selfie and a snap of JR behind the wheel of a car.

    Quincy captioned the post: "drama_king858 is a surprisingly laid back guy."

    She is the only daughter of RHONY star Sonja, 57.

    The TV personality shares Quincy with ex-husband John Adams Morgan, who she was married to from 1998-2006.

    Similar to Quincy, Sonja has also been known to share snaps of herself in bikinis on social media.

    Earlier this month, the Bravo star took to Instagram to show off some major side boob as she posed in a tiny swimsuit.
