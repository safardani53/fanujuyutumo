    HALLOWEEN fans have voiced their excitement about a new lineup of spooky snacks.

    In the spirit of the season, PepsiCo has dressed up some of its most popular products for Halloween.

    The new items start with the most important meal of the day - breakfast.

    Cap'n Crunch Halloween Crunch swaps out the Crunch Berries for ghosts that turn the milk green to get people in a ghoulish mood.

    Those who prefer a warm start to the day can enjoy the fall flavors of Quaker's pumpkin spice and cinnamon spice oatmeal. Or for a snack there are Quaker Apple Cinnamon rice cakes.

    The Cheetos Bag of Bones is back for another year with two flavor options; white cheddar and cinnamon sugar.

    read more on food and drink

    Fans keep their eye out for the famous Bag of Bones each year, and this year the cinnamon sugar option has been a huge hit.

    "Frito Lay, the bag of bones cinnamon Cheetos is the best things y'all ever did. Y'all needa keep those around," wrote on user on X, formerly known as Twitter.

    Another added: "I'm now addicted to the bag of bones Cheetos."

    The Cheetos fun continues with the Mac 'N Cheese Box of Bones.

    Most read in Money

    Available in the Bold and Cheesy flavor, it has the same taste as the original Cheetos Mac 'N Cheese with the fun addition of pasta shaped like bones and skulls.

    Finally, there is the Quaker Chewy Halloween Minis, which features granola bars made with real chocolate chips and colorful sprinkles.

    FRIGHTENING FUN

    There seems to be an endless amount of Halloween-themed foods coming out this year.

    Dunkin' just launched its iconic Spider Donut, which features orange frosting with a glazed chocolate munchkin in the center decorated with eyes and creepy black legs.

    On X, one person wrote: "Had a bad day until I just remembered that the spider donuts are back at Dunkin'."

    The donut shop launched its initial fall menu back in August, featuring the iconic pumpkin spice lattee.

    Also included in the lineup are the glazed pumpkin cake donut and the Munchkin donut hole treats, as well as the pumpkin muffin which is topped with streusel and a drizzle of white icing.

    Customers can also order the Nutty Pumpkin coffee, which features hazelnut and cream.
