    THE Kardashians seem to have a foot fetish.

    The reality TV family members, who frequently show off their bodies in skimpy swimsuits and bikinis, also seem to have a love for their feet.

    Get a leg up on the Kardashians' obsession with feet.

    KOURTNEY KARDASHIAN

    Perhaps no sister puts her best foot forward more often than Kourtney.

    The newlywed and her husband, Travis Barker, have never been shy about showing off their love, and that includes some feet PDA

    Last year, the rocker and reality star transformed themselves into Alabama and Clarence from the iconic film, True Romance.

    The pair dressed up like Christian Slater and Patricia Arquette's characters in the movie.

    In the photos, the Poosh founder sat on the trunk of a convertible while Travis sucked on her bare foot.

    During last year's holiday season, Travis kissed Kourtney's foot and posted about it on Instagram.

    He captioned the post: "All I want for Christmas."

    Kourtney replied in the comments: "You've been extra good."

    The couple also honored one of the most influential foot moments of all time when they reenacted the climactic scene from Cinderella.

    In the close up shot posted to her Instagram Stories, Kourtney portrayed Cinderella by holding her foot out while her soon-to-be husband slid her heel on.

    She included a screengrab from the famed animated scene where the prince fits Cinderella with her glass slipper, mimicking the romantic moment.

    Similarly, when she launched her new vitamin gummies brand Lemme in September, Kourtney couldn't resist adding a close-up of her toes in a promotional video.

    She showed off her painted toenails and the natural wrinkles on her feet as the reality star-turned-entrepreneur embraces aging.

    KIM KARDASHIAN

    Khloe once hilariously called out sister Kim after she and her then-boyfriend Pete Davidson went toe-to-toe in a series of NSFW photos.

    One photo in the bunch showed the reality star lying with her feet up against Pete's chest.

    Khloe had plenty to say about the snap, commenting: "Do you guys have a foot fetish too?" in reference to Kourtney and Travis.

    After years of speculation, Kim finally showed off her feet in 2021, and debunked the wild rumors that she has SIX toes.

    The reality star gave evidence that she only has five toes on each foot during an April 2021 episode of Keeping Up With the Kardashians.

    While looking at her feet through the camera on her phone, the KUWTK star said: "Guys, everyone thinks I have six toes."

    As she showed off her feet and sandals, she continued: "It's really kind of wild."

    Kim then counted her toes and pointed out the side of her foot next to her pinky toe, adding: "But then it's this part of my foot. It like smashes down right here.

    "And like in the picture, I don't know why, it looks like a sixth toe."

    The reality star concluded: "Very baffling."

    Kim has been accused of having six toes multiple times, as fans most recently made the claim after she posted a photo from her controversial birthday party in October.

    The strange conspiracy involving the reality star began in 2019, when fans believed they spotted a bonus toe on her left foot.

    Back in September, Kim was forced to address speculation over her feet on Instagram after fans claimed they spotted an extra toe.

    In a series of videos about her feet, Kim said: "Everyone thinks that I have six toes, and it's really wild."

    After counting her five toes, the Skims founder explained that what people believed was an extra toe is just the side part of her foot.

    She continued: "But it's this, part of my foot, that when I wear a shoe just like this it like smashes down right here and in a picture.

    "I don't know why it looks like a sixth toe."

    KYLIE JENNER

    Kylie was forced to defend her "weird a**" toes after fans spotted something unique when she shared in a series of bikini shots.

    The billionaire, 24, showed off her curves in stunning snaps in 2020, but followers couldn't help but notice that her middle toe was shorter than the others.

    After legions of fans commented on how strange they thought the toe was, Kylie was forced to reveal her "weird a**" toe was like that because of an injury.

    Speaking on her Instagram Stories, she explained: "So I broke this middle toe in middle school. There's nothing you can do for a broken toe, so I just had to let it heal how it wanted to heal."

    She continued: "So when I lift this [left foot] up they're normal and like in place.

    "And when I flex this [right foot] up this little guy is like out of place."

    However, at the end she clarified: "By the way I have cute a** feet."

    Kylie went one step further when she jokingly named all of her little piggies.

    In an Instagram video, the star focused on her feet, giving each of them special names: Tess, Teague, Tia, Trix, Toby, Tony, Tahnee, Tasha, Talia, and Tina.

    The make-up mogul also decided to mix up the genders, telling fans the “big toes are boys.”

    “My toes are so famous,” she wrote.

    "So we probably should name them at this point.”

    KENDALL JENNER

    Fans have also noticed an "odd" detail about Kendall Jenner's feet in her vacation photos.

    Kendall, 26, shared snapshots from her tropical getaway on Instagram.

    Kardashians fans claimed in an online thread that the snaps were "photoshopped" as the model's toes appeared "odd and long."

    One fan said: "I couldn’t help but notice how long her toes are."

    Another fan said: "Her toe length is freaking me out."

    A third fan mentioned: "Girl is like ten feet tall. Of course, her fingers and toes will be long."
