    Their secret affair happened behind-the-scenes of a movie as they reportedly held hands on set.

    While pretending to have a friendship in public, Elliot said they would leave hotels at different exits while dodging the paparazzi.

    THE SPLIT

    In January 2021, the Juno star filed for divorce after three years of marriage with ex-wife Emma Portner, 28.

    According to documents obtained by TMZ, the former couple asked for a dissolution of their marriage.

    THE TRANSITION

    In November 2020, Elliot shared a lengthy Twitter post and revealed he is transgender.

    The Oscar-nominated actor confirmed his new pronouns and tweeted: "I feel lucky to be writing this. To be here. To have arrived at this place in my life."

    He went on: "I'm scared of the invasiveness, the hate, the 'jokes,' and of violence. 

    "To be clear, I am not trying to dampen a moment that is joyous, and one that I celebrate, but I want to address the full picture."

    The X-Men: Days of Future Past star thanked the trans community for their "courage, generosity, and ceaselessly working to make this world a more inclusive and compassionate place."
