    How to Pick the Right One-Piece Swimsuit 

    Fit

    Above all else, your one-piece needs to feel snug and comfortable. Look for materials that compress and stretch (like nylon and spandex). You want your suit to stay in place, whether you’re exercising in the pool, playing water games with the family, or lounging on the beach. Always check the brand’s specific sizing guide, and if you are between sizes, look for suits with adjustable straps or other features for a more customized fit. When in doubt, size up. Some suits come in various lengths for long torsos, so we promise a well-fitted swimsuit is possible.

    Activity or Purpose

    When shopping for your one-piece, think about what purpose it’ll serve. Are you lounging poolside or swimming laps? “For training and athletic wear, I always choose a tighter, more compressed fit, while for lounging and beach wear, I go for more of a comfortable, relaxed fit,” says Weyant. Certain suits like Weyant’s Micro Back One Piece are designed with chlorine-resistant fabric specifically for swimmers. If you're just looking to lounge poolside, an affordable pick like the Cupshe V-Neck One-Piece Bathing Suit may be all you need. If you want to do it all (swim, lounge, and keep up with the kids), our best overall pick, The Amalfi, won't let you down.

    Style

    Weyant thinks finding a “style that makes you feel confident every time you put it on” is key when choosing a one-piece. With an abundance of styles available, there’s bound to be one that floats your boat. Certain styles will highlight different features. Show off your legs in Aerie’s Shine Pique Babewatch One-Piece. Flex your back in Cupshe’s V-Neck Lace Up One-Piece. The best style is one that makes you feel great in your own skin.

    Price

    The pricing of one-pieces varies greatly. Our picks range from $30 to $350 — it’s just a matter of personal budget. Maybe you’re able to invest in one luxury piece or would rather snag several affordable suits. You may also want to spend more on a suit with certain features like sustainable materials including recycled nylon. No matter your budget, there’s a great one-piece for you. 

    Frequently Asked Questions

    • How do I know what size one-piece swimsuit to buy?

      Swimsuit brands often include a size guide that details the specific measurements for each size, but it will require you to take your own measurements. By taking your own bust, waist, and hip measurements, you can compare with the brand’s sizing guide to find the appropriate fit. As you're shopping around places to buy swimsuits, it’s a good idea to do this for each brand, as sizing can differ by brand.

    • What one-piece swimsuit is in style for 2023?

    • Where are the best places to buy one-piece swimsuits?

      Major retailers like Target and Amazon are popular places to buy swimsuits, thanks to their affordability and flexible return policies. Online brands like Summersalt and Andie are also popular options. They both offer extensive sizing, fit guides, and customer reviews to help guide your buying process.
