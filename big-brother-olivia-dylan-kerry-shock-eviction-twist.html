    BIG Brother fans have been left stunned as three housemates face eviction TONIGHT.

    During tonight's show viewers watched as the next shopping task got underway with a Halloween theme.

    Housemates were haunted by ex-stars Farida, Zac and Hallie before one contestant was given a secret task.

    Big Brother called one housemate to the diary room, with Noky being the first to answer the request.

    She then learned she had been possessed and would have to pick another housemate to help her on the mission.

    Noky chose Trish, with the pair having to place three housemates up for eviction.

    In the guise of the night they chose Olivia, Dylan and Kerry to face a flash vote.

    At the end of the episode the vote opened for the public to decide who stays and who goes.

    It will close during Late and Live with AJ Odudu confirming the housemate with the most vote being booted out the backdoor tonight.

    However, viewers will have to tune in to Tuesday's episode to find out who was given their marching orders.
