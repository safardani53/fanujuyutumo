    DANCE Gavin Dance is a popular rock band that formed in 2005.

    In 2022, the band will be hitting the road for a new tour, and now fans want to know where they can get their tickets.

    Dance Gavin Dance 2022 tour: How can I buy tickets?

    Tickets for Dance Gavin Dance's tour are on sale now.

    They can be purchased through the band's official website or on Ticketmaster.

    Prices start at $50 plus taxes and fees. Vip packages are also available for fans.

    Who are the members of Dance Gavin Dance?

    Since forming in 2005, Dance Gavin Dance has released nine studio albums as well as a number of instrumental albums.

    In March 2022, they also debuted the new song, Synergy, featuring Rob Damiani.

    Members of the band currently consist of Tilian Pearson, Jon Mess, Will Swan, Andrew Wells, Tim Feerick, and Matthew Mingus.
