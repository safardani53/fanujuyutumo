    FRESH off her Las Vegas Residency, Shania Twain is scheduled to hit the road again.

    The GRAMMY Award winner is headlining stadiums all over the US for her Queen of Me Tour.

    How can I buy tickets to Shania Twain's Queen of Me Tour?

    On November 3, 2022, Shania Twain announced she was hitting several cities for the promotion of her forthcoming album Queen of Me.

    That Thursday at 10am PST, tickets went on presale through Ticketmaster, Bands in Town, and Live Nation.

    On Friday, November 4, the presale continued as fans attempted to score their own tickets for Shania's 2023 concerts.

    While VIP packages range from $517 to $863, other admission prices ranged from $85 for the farthest sections and about $390 for seats closer to the stage.

    The tour reveal follows the release of Shania's song Waking Up Dreaming, the lead single from her Queen of Me album.

    The record is her sixth album overall and is her first since her full-length 2017 project Now.

    Prior to her embarking on another concert circuit, she concluded her Let's Go! residency in Las Vegas months before on September 10, 2022.

    What is Shania's net worth?

    During the 1990s, Shania rose to music stardom for her hits You're Still the One, From This Moment On, That Don't Impress Me Much, and Man! I Feel Like A Woman.

    In November 1997, her third album Come On Over was made available to listeners everywhere and went on to become the greatest-selling country music album of all time.

    With this achievement, she supplanted previous record-holders The Dixie Chicks and Garth Brooks.

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, Shania amassed an estimated fortune of $400million.

    On June 2, 2011, she was honored with her own star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
