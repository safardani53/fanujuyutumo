    ROCHELLE Humes has shared a glimpse into her daughters’ adorable wardrobes, with the mum-of-two a fan of high street bargains. 

    Rochelle, 31, recently began sharing snaps of her kids, Alaia, seven, and Valentina, three, on Instagram - and the adorable outfits she dresses them in. 

    The former Saturdays singer is a big fan of high street names including Zara and River Island, along with eco-friendly brands. 

    Her 1.9 million followers have been gushing over the girls’ chic clothes, desperate to copy their style. 

    Rochelle, who’s expecting her third child, a boy, with husband Marvin Humes, 35, loves chic blazers, coats, summery dresses and slogan tops for her little ones. 

    Sharing a snap earlier today of Alaia in a bright dress, paired with a straw hat and round shades, the presenter said: “Picked her own outfit, she looks like she should be in the South of France somewhere, into this. Be more Lai.” 

    The youngster wore a similar hat with a summery pink frock last month, which fans were also desperate to snap up. 

    For anyone keen to kit their kids out in a similar dress, Rochelle said: “Zara, last summer though.” 

    The mum is also thought to have snapped up a chic cardigan from Zara, after sharing a snap of Valentina wearing the pastel-hued chunky knit. 

    Fans eagerly tracked down the knitwear, with one mum commenting: “It's Zara my little girl has same one, got it in winter tho.”

    River Island is another go-to shop for Rochelle, with Valentina rocking a adorable white polka dot shirred dress last month, paired with matching espadrilles. 

    An eagle-eyed mum confirmed the set was from the high street shop, commenting: “She’s gorgeous!!!!

    "Love that little set #riverislandkids.”

    The youngster wore another polka-dot creation from the store, which went down a treat with fans after Rochelle paired the slogan top with some fluffy sliders. 

    Another fashion fan identified the top, saying: “It’s River Island I think.”
