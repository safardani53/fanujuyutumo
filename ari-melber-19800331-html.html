    Ari Naftali Melber is an American lawyer and journalist, chief legal reporter for MSNBC and host The Beat with Ari Melber. The show premiered on July 24, 2017 and was nominated for the 2020 Emmy Award for Excellent Live Interview. In January 2021, The Beat topped the audience chart at 6 p.m., averaging 2.6 million viewers per night, and topped CNN and FOX News. The Beat rating marks "the best MSNBC rating for the period." Melber's show has become one of the most watched online shows, with "13 million viewers per month on YouTube - the highest of any MSNBC show," according to the Daily Beast. Beat Reports are among the most viewed online segments in MSNBC history. Columbia Journalism Review calls Melber "a remarkably effective interviewer", while New York Times reporter Peter Wehner calls him "an excellent interviewer, among the best on television."
