    A MODEL on The Price is Right has interrupted the game show and caused Drew Carey to ensure all was well.

    Manuela Arbalaez took off her shoe to deal with a pest on air.

    Drew, 65, explained a round called the Bargain Game to a Price is Right contestant on stage.

    As he was speaking, Manuela cut him off and yelled: "Oh my god" from her podium.

    The brunette beauty donned a glittery low-cut blue gown and black high heels.

    One of which she then removed to smack something on the lectern.

    MEET THE MODELS

    The Price is Right tasks its audience members with guessing the nearest price of various products presented by a handful of models who have become familiar faces over the years.

    Born in Medellín, Colombia, Manuela became a finalist on The Price is Right Model Search in 2008, joining the show the following year.

    Manuella also appeared in The Bold and the Beautiful and was a model at the 54th Grammy Awards.

    Manuela has been married to Matthew Doherty since February 17, 2018; they share two children.

    Meanwhile, Rachel is the longest-serving model on The Price is Right, joining the show in 2003.

    Before her time on the game show, Rachel attended Lousiana State University, where she was a member of the track team.

    Rachel married her longtime boyfriend, former MLB outfielder David Dellucci in 2010; they share one child.

    Season 52 of The Price Is Right is the first season at a new studio.

    It's also the first since the death of longtime host Bob Barker - premiering on September 25.

    The Price Is Right left Television City after 50 years for a new location in nearby Glendale, L.A.

    LOSS OF A LEGEND

    Bob died on the morning of Saturday, August 26, 2023, in his Los Angeles home - at the time it was said to be due to natural causes.

    He was 99 at the time of his death.

    Bob began hosting The Price is Right in 1972 and held an impressive 35-year run before his retirement.

    Due to his passion for animal rights, his iconic tagline at the end of each episode was: “Help control the pet population.

    "Have your pet spayed or neutered."

    The iconic television star's achievements include 19 Emmys, a Guinness World Records award, and being inducted into the Television Academy Hall of Fame.
