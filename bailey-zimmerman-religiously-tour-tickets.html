    RISING country singer Bailey Zimmerman will be hitting the road in 2024 for a new tour.

    Now, fans want to know how they can secure their tickets to his upcoming shows.

    How can I get tickets to Bailey Zimmerman's 2024 Religiously tour?

    Bailey Zimmerman announced on June 12, 2023, that he will be hitting the road with special guest Josh Ross in 2024.

    Presale tickets are set to go on sale June 14 at 10am local time with general public tickets set to be released two days later.

    Fans can purchase tickets directly on Zimmerman's website or on Ticketmaster.

    Where is Bailey Zimmerman performing?

    Zimmerman will be hitting the road in February 2024 following the conclusion of Morgan Wallen's One Night At A Time World Tour.

    Who is Bailey Zimmerman?

    Zimmerman is a country music artist who was born on January 27, 2000, in Louisville, Illinois.

    He first rose to fame in 2022 following the release of his singles Fall in Love and Rock and a Hard Place, which both reached No. 1 on the Billboard Country Airplay chart.

    Before Zimmerman was touring with high-profile stars and releasing albums, he was posting content on social media to thousands of followers.

    “The TikTok and the Instagram, me doing that every day, that is the brand, of course,” Zimmerman explained during an interview with The New York Times.

    “That is the company. And now it’s like, whoa, whoa, whoa, label, hold on. I now have an avenue of my own to make me successful by myself.” 

    Zimmerman is among many stars who owe their careers to TikTok, joining the likes of Olivia Rodrigo, Måneskin, and Jax, who were all discovered on social media.
