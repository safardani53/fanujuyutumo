    CHANNEL 5'S The Catch has had viewers hooked, with it's gripping storyline.

    The drama brought with it Welsh rising star, Aneurin Bernard.

    Who is Aneurin Barnard?

    Born in Glamorgan, Wales, on May 8, 1987, Aneurin Bernard and is a stage and screen actor.

    In 2010 he cemented his career in the acting world when he won an Olivier Award for the coming-of-age musical Spring Awakening.

    In 2023 it was announced that Aneurin will be joining the cast of Doctor Who.

    He has been enlisted to play Roger ap Gwilliam, the mysterious leader of a political party called Albion.

    What has Aneurin Barnard starred in?

    During his time in the spotlight so far, he has appeared on both the big and small screen.

    In the movies he has starred in David Bailey biopic We'll Take Manhattan, Christopher Nolan’s war epic Dunkirk, and Donna Tartt film adaptation The Goldfinch.

    He also appeared as Mozart in 2017 flick Interlude in Prague, and Paul Langevin in 2019's Radioactive.

    Over on TV he is best known for playing Tommy Shelby’s mysterious Doctor Holford in Peaky Blinders.

    Most read in Drama

    In 2013, he played King Richard III in The White Queen.

    He played Bobby in TV mini series Cilla and John Trenchard in Moonfleet, and he starred in 2016 drama, Thirteen.

    In 2021, he starred as Jack in the first season of The Pact.

    Who does Aneurin Barnard play in The Catch?

    The Catch follows father and local fisherman, Ed, who is determined to keep his family together, after they suffered tragedy.

    Aneurin plays Ryan - a rich mysterious younger man who becomes Ed's daughter Abbie's boyfriend.

    As a protective father, Ed wants to uncover the truth about Ryan before it's too late.

    Is Aneurin Barnard on social media?

    Aneurin is active on social media, and likes to keep his fans up-to-date with his acting work.

    Over on Instagram, you can join his 96k followers on his handle @aneurin.b.

    You can also keep up-to-date with Aneurin on Twitter, @aneurinBarnard, where he has 55,000 followers.

    All social media stats are correct as of January 2023.
