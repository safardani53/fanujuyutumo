    Lifetime’s Dance Moms Season 4 Episode 26 saw a trip to Florida and the continuation of Abby’s war with the moms, which boiled over last week.

    The competition – the last before nationals – is Dance Kids USA in Orlando. But first, Holly has some beef with Abby that she would like to straighten out.

    Abby sees the mom’s criticism as trying to excuse her daughter Nia from bad dancing, and refuses to give an inch. Holly won’t back down either.

    It’s intense and we’ve barely even begun to watch Dance Moms online.

    As the girls arrive on Dance Moms Season 4 Episode 26, Abby explains how she pulled last week’s number because of the moms, leaving no doubt where she stands.

    Maddie is on top this week, because she danced last week. Period.

    The tension is continuing to build with the angry moms; Abby tells the girls that if the moms go, they go. Holly says she’s going to "hold Abby accountable."

     

    Abby’s group dance this week will feature the girls as the seven dwarfs. Musical theater student Kaylee is called in for the number, to the dismay or Maddie.

    One mom, Kate, asks about Kaylee’s chances on the team. Mistake.

    Abby tells her it’s none of her business. Holly, meanwhile, is forced to admit that Abby’s white board was a good idea and that the rehearsal went well.

    The coach tells her young charges that there will be zero sun and zero fun until after the competition in Orlando. She is tough as nails, this woman.

    As the girls prepare for the group dance, Kate criticizes the solos. Abby looks stunned and is also upset that Cathy touched her during the group routine.

    Sarah’s routine places fourth. Kendall gets second. Nia is first.

    Abby thinks Nia did a great job. Kendall understands she should have done better. Abby lets Sarah go, which makes her cry and upsets Tracey.

    In an epic twist, Cathy waltzes in, calls the routine out as dumb and tells everyone that at Nationals, they have something amazing planned, so take heed.

    Cathy and Abby exchange words, and Miller takes the heated beef to a new level altogether by threatening to get Cathy kicked out of competition. Oooh.
