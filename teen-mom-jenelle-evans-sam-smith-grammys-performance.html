    JENELLE Evans has angered fans after admitting she didn't like Sam Smith's unique performance at the Grammy Awards.

    The reality star voiced her seemingly unpopular opinion during an Instagram Q&A on Tuesday.

    In the post, Jenelle, 31, shared a selfie of her staring wide-eyed at the camera while donning eyeglasses and her newly dyed blonde locks.

    Above it, she shared a fan's comment about her previous reaction to the Stay With Me singer's performance.

    "Glad to see you liking the post calling out the demonic performance at the Grammys," the remark read, referring to Sam's on-air moment.

    Jenelle echoed that she was not a fan, commenting: "That s**t has me like..."

    Sam took the Grammys stage on Sunday night with Kim Petras to perform their hit track, Unholy.

    They indeed put on a show for the crowd as Sam rocked bright red spandex, and a matching top hat with horns, while Kim also sported a sexy red number.

    They each added their own interesting flare, with Kim appearing inside a cage as dancers with whips danced around her.

    Sam also didn't hold back, making suggestive faces at the audience, giving arguably the raciest performance of the night.

    'SHE BETTER NOT'

    While the audience went wild for the sexy rendition, clearly not everyone was pleased, including ex-Teen Mom 2 star Jenelle.

    However, many fans disagreed and bashed the MTV alum for her remark in an online thread.

    "Is this girl coming for Sam Smith???? She better not," one person wrote.

    "Dude wore a Party City devil costume. It’s not that serious. I’ve seen worse on Halloween which, if I remember correctly, she wholeheartedly participates in," another said.

    "I love that Sam Smith pissed off exactly who he wanted to with that performance. Like that was the whole point. How are people so simple minded," chimed in a third.

    "I don’t think I’ve ever rolled my eyes so hard," someone else added.

    ONLYFANS

    Jenelle has been sharing her own racy content on social media in recent months while continuing to promote her OnlyFans account.

    Earlier this week, Jenelle provided details on what to expect on her "spicy site" while responding to a fan question on her Instagram Stories.

    The heavily filtered ex-TV star was shown making a kissy face from the front seat of her car as she replied to the inquiry.

    In the "Ask me a question" field, a follower begged: "Foot content on your OF," followed by the "praying hands" emoji.

    The fan's prayers were quickly answered as Jenelle responded: "Just [got] my toes painted for y'all."

    Fans again raged against the mom of three on an online forum, with one writing: "People are actually asking for content?" followed by a nauseous face emoji.

    Another person wrote: "The idea of OF content featuring the big goon monster David is," completing the sentence with a trail of "vomiting" emojis.

    "I know I say this every day, but she is so gross," stated one sickened fan.

    A second commenter questioned: "Lol has she ever posted feet stuff on OF? Lol I feel like she asked herself that question."

    While a third speculated: "She 100% sent these questions to herself lol."

    CLAPPING BACK

    Jenelle previously hit back at critics after facing backlash for promoting her OnlyFans account.

    The TV personality has been receiving comments across social media about her chosen method of making money as of late.

    One critic commented, "No but for real, if she was a responsible adult like she claims she is then she wouldn't have to self-promote her 'side job.'"

    However, Jenelle quickly clapped back in a TikTok video responding to the comment.

    Sitting on her bed in her glasses with her long tresses around her shoulders, Jenelle offered her reasoning for keeping an OnlyFans account while clad in black pajamas.

    "If I was a responsible adult, I'd be paying my bills, right?" asked Jenelle.

    "I'd be making money to pay my bills. Seems like I'm doing that, right?"

    The Teen Mom alum continued: "There are so many different ways to make money these days, so to me promoting and doing promotions for Chinese products that are gonna be broken by the time they get to my customers? No, I'm not doing that. I'm not gonna fake social media for my fans."

    She explained further: "So I'm gonna find a different way to make money, and if that's a spicy site, more power to me, right? I have to pay taxes on this. It's a real job, believe it or not. So while you're sitting here hating, I'm gonna be laying on my bed, making money."
