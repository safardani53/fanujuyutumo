    A REAL Housewives of Beverly Hills astrologer told Denise Richards she has the "most to hide."

    The revelation in a promo for this week's episode comes as Denise has been shutting down Brandi Glanville affair rumors.

    In a promo scene for this week's episode an astrologer, Shawn, told Denise: "a very intensely complicated chart."

    She responded she's actually heard this before.

    Shawn continues: "Aquarians are very friendly, they're open and then, because of the Scorpion moon, you're also very loving.

    "But the thing is, there is a side of you that is gonna be hidden. So, of anyone, the one that will have the most to reveal will be you."

    For RHOBH fans this is most likely in reference to Brandi's rumored affair with Denise.

    The two were apparently together from early 2019 through the middle of last year.

    Denise had reportedly told Brandi during this time she and her husband, Aaron Phypers, were in an open marriage.

    However, the mom of three has refuted these claims.

    Denise told ET last week: "If it plays out the way it was shot, people will see [my side of things], and if there's questions, obviously I'll answer it at the reunion."

    She also confirmed last month she would be at the taping, although the cast had expected her not to attend.

    Denise told HollywoodLife: “The only people who’ve said that I’m not going to the reunion are some of the ladies on the show, and they never asked me if I was going or not.

    She went on: “I’m planning on going if we still have [it]. I don’t know how we’re going to be doing the reunion now.

    Denise went on: "Maybe things will be lifted, and we’ll be able to do it, even if it’s on Zoom.

    "But I never said that I wasn’t going to be there. They’re the ones that are saying that."

    The season premier saw Kyle Richards say to Lisa Rinna of Denise: “I bet you anything she doesn’t show up for the reunion.”

    Lisa responded: “Oh, she’s done. If you ask me, we would never see Denise Richards again."
