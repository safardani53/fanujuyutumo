    18 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Real Housewives of Orange County‘s Meghan King Edmonds continues to share heart-warming moments of her family after the birth of her daughter Aspen King Edmonds on Thanksgiving Day.

    On Saturday, the new mom took to Instagram to post an adorable photo of her husband Jim Edmonds holding baby Aspen.

    “Somebody’s got daddy wrapped around their little finger already,” King Edmonds, 32, wrote in the post showing her husband giving his newborn daughter a kiss.

    Aspen joins Edmonds’ four children from his previous relationships.

    King Edmonds announced the birth on Instagram last Friday, just one day after the couple welcomed their 6 lb., 11 oz. healthy daughter.

    Last Saturday, the reality star followed up with more information on the birth, including her daughter’s name, writing on Instagram, “From the hours leading up to the birth I had the most incredible husband, he didn’t leave my side. I could not have labored naturally without him and my doula.”
