    MANCHESTER UNITED, Arsenal and Liverpool are all battling it out for MK Dons starlet Dylan Asonganyi.

    The Under-18s ace, 16, has already scored nine times in just seven games for the League One outfit's Under-18s so far in 2017-18.

    Asonganyi is also on the radar of Slaven Bilic's West Ham.

    However, MK Dons are unlikely to allow their star youngster walk away on the cheap.

    The forward, who turns 17 in December, has been tipped to make his senior bow in the coming months for the League One side.

    Asonganyi has also excelled progressing through England's youth ranks in recent months - having taken part in the Under-16s' training camp aged just 13.

    Should Asonganyi leave the club, he would be the second high-profile mover to switch Milton Keynes for the Premier League.
