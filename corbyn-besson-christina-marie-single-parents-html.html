    Musician Corbyn Besson and Christina Harris, a YouTuber known as "BeautyChickee," who were in a relationship for a long time, have broken up.

    To confirm the split, they even recorded a video together and posted it on their social media in January 2021. The video is now available to watch on a fangirl YouTube and Instagram handle.

    In the 38-second video, Harris addressed the rumors of their separation that were going around by stating that the rumors were true. 

    "...wanted to come here quicky and just address the rumors that yes we did break up," Harris said.

    She also revealed that she and Besson had separated "towards the beginning of the quarantine." And both of them did not mention that news with their fans because they wanted some time for themselves to heal.

    ...we didn't mention anything about it because we just wanted some time like for ourselves and just time to heal.

    The talented singer-songwriter also agreed with what his ex-girlfriend had to say about their break-up. Doing that, he also stated that the two were on good terms and that they were still friends.

    Besson stated, "Before there's any like extra drama and speculation...uh...we're on good terms, we're friends."

    Although the video confirmed that Besson and Harris had actually separated, fans were saddened to know the truth.

    "tbh i’ve never been this sad over a social media couple breaking up, but i’m super glad that they’re both on really good terms," a fan commented on YouTube.

    Another wrote in disbelief, saying, "Wait, it’s real?!?!?? I don’t believe in love."

    Besson and Harris's fans had such an emotion attached to the former couple because they were together for very long and seemed perfect together.

    Corbyn Besson and his ex-girlfriend Christina Marie Harris acting silly together in a picture before the breakup. (Photo: Christina's Instagram)

    The former couple hadn't mentioned where and when they first met each other, but they had disclosed that they started dating in 2016.

    More importantly, Harris was Besson's first girlfriend, too.

    Many even wanted them to get married and maintain a family life - but it seems a tough ask now. As of now, Besson seems to be enjoying his single life and focuses on his career.

    Besson’s Bio Reveals His Parents, Age, Height, and Net Worth

    The pop singer-songwriter Besson was born on November 25, 1998, in Texas, the United States. His height is exactly 5 feet and 10 inches.

    He is the eldest of the three children born to his parents. He was raised in Virginia alongside his two siblings Jordan and Ashley.

    His father's name is Ray Besson, a Senior Program Manager at Amazon Logistics Technology Integration.

    Corbyn Besson revealed the identity of his parents (father & mother) through his Instagram. (Photo: Corbyn Besson's Instagram) 

    On Besson's 12th birthday, he received a guitar as a gift from his parents which later on helped him build a passion for singing, songwriting, and creating music.

    Slowly, his creative skills on music boosted his confidence, and he started uploading his music on social media. 

    Today, Besson is one of the members of the boyband “Why Don’t We.” Most of the fortunes come in his life from his music career and endorsement deals.

    According to Celebrity Net Worth, he possesses a net worth of $2 million to date. He also owns a BMW car, which he had gifted himself on his 21st birthday in December 2019.
