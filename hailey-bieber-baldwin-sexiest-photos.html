    Hailey Baldwin is a true trendsetter. Celebrate her 27th birthday with a look back at some of her hottest photos ever!

    Hailey Bieber (nèe Baldwin) really is one of the fashion world’s favorites. The blonde beauty is an American model, the daughter of actor Stephen Baldwin, and the niece of Alec Baldwin. Hailey started out her modeling career as a teen, befriending A-listers Kendall Jenner and Gigi Hadid. In July 2022, she moved on to the beauty business with her Rhode skincare line. And it seems like ladies are willing to take any style tip from Hailey, be it manicure colors or the latest lip gloss.

    Hailey is also known for her relationship with husband Justin Bieber. They briefly dated at the beginning of 2016, but their romance fizzled out not long after. But after reconnecting in June 2018, things happened fast.

    A month after reuniting, Justin proposed to Hailey on July 7, 2018, while on a trip to the Bahamas. They went to an NYC Marriage Bureau on Sept. 13 of that year and secretly tied the knot. It wasn’t until a year later that they celebrated with their friends and loved ones at a real wedding in South Carolina. So sweet.
