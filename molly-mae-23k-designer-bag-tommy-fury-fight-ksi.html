    Molly-Mae shows off incredible 23k designer bag as she watches boyfriend Tommy Fury fight KSI

    MOLLY-Mae Hague turned up at boyfriend Tommy Fury's fight at the weekend with a 23,000 handbag. The former Love Island star made sure to glam up as she watched her husband clash with the influencer in the ring.

    26 Mar 2024, 00:00

    MOLLY-Mae Hague turned up at boyfriend Tommy Fury's fight at the weekend with a £23,000 handbag.

    The former Love Island star made sure to glam up as she watched her husband clash with the influencer in the ring.

    Molly, 24, took to social media at various points during the evening to flaunt her glam outfit.

    She mostly wore a chic black ensemble which included a floor length bodycon dress and a smart blazer cloak.

    However the former PrettyLittleThing creative director added a pop of colour with her small green designer handbag.

    In one snap, she clutched the stunning accessory as she posed in the hallway of the pair's huge £3.5million mansion.

    From the snapshots of the evening, it looks as though Molly donned the Hermes mini Kelly bag.

    According to auction house Sotheby's the accessory is lined with tonal agneau lambskin leather and is worth around £23,000 ($28,000).

    Over the weekend, Tommy Fury and KSI's highly-anticipated fight took place in Manchester on October 14, 2023.

    The exact figure the former Love Island star made from his controversial clash is not known.

    He said before the fight: "It's easy money. Anybody else in my position wouldn't turn it down. Even world champions.

    "These YouTube fights are not my highest point in life. I'm here to do a lot more than that."
