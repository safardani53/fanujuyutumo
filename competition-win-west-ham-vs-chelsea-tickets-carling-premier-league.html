    14 Apr 2024, 00:00

    SUNSPORT has teamed up with Carling – the official beer and cider of the Premier League – to give one fan the chance to win FOUR tickets in the home end for West Ham v Chelsea at the London stadium on Sunday September 23 (1.30pm kick-off).

    To be in with a shout, simply answer this simple question:

    In which country was West Ham boss Manuel Pellegrini born?

    Email your answer, with your contact details and date of birth to sportsquiz-sm@the-sun.co.uk by noon on Thursday September 20.

    Entrants must be aged 18 or over.

    Throughout the Premier League season, Carling will be giving away tickets to groups of mates, as well as a whole host of other footy-related prizes.

    Carling’s new ‘For the Fans’ campaign involves match-day squads hitting venues across the country to elevate the pub experience.

    Landlords will hand over the Carling Goal of the Month Award for the first time and the ‘They Score, You Score’ game returns to the Carling Tap App after another successful season.
