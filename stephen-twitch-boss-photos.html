    Stephen ‘tWitch’ Boss is being remembered after his shocking death at 40, one year ago today. See photos here.

    Stephen “tWitch” Boss, DJ for The Ellen Show, died at 40 on December 13, 2022, by an apparent suicide. After his wife found he had left home without his car, she reported it to police. He was found dead by a self-inflicted gunshot at a local hotel not long after. tWitch was beloved at the D.J. on the Ellen show. He started his run on the series in 2014 and was there until the last episode in 2022. He would even take on guest hosting and was promoted to an Executive Producer in 2020. He is survived by his wife Allison Holker, whom he married in 2013. The couple had 3 kids. To remember Stephen tWitch Boss, check out our gallery here.
