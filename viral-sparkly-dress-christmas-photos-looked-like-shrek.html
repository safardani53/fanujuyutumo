    08 May 2024, 00:00

    A BUDDING fashionista was in for a nasty surprise when she tried on a dress she had hoped to wear for Christmas.

    Her hopes were dashed when she realized that the green sequinned dress made her look like Shrek, instead of like a princess.

    Abi Morton (@abimortonnn) acquired the outfit in question after being inspired by a viral video.

    In the clip with 3.5 million views, influencer Collins Tuohy Smith wore the grass-green, long-sleeved sequinned dress while decorating a Christmas tree.

    Abi hoped to recreate the magic for her festive family snaps, but she was sorely disappointed.

    Her video was entitled: "Point of view: me trying on said green dress for the first time that I had to have for family Christmas photos."

    The clip showed Abi looking at herself in the mirror for the first time while wearing the dress.

    She had a horrified expression and overlaid text read that she was "shooketh."

    "I look like Shrek," Abi said as she sighed.

    Her caption read: "The outfit did not look the same in person as it did in my head."

    Commenters were quick to chime in with their thoughts.

    "I actually really like it on you! No one looks great in any dress without doing a little something something to make it all come together," one person wrote.

    "We had such high hopes for this dress," another said, to which Abi replied: "Immediately returned hahaha."

    "A shrek slay," someone else wrote.
