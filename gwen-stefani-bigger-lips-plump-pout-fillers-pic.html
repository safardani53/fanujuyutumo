    Has Gwen Stefani become the latest star to jump on the plumped up pout bandwagon? The 48-year-old was photographed heading into a LA studio on Sept. 4 and there was no mistaking that her lips appeared much larger than usual. From a side view, her upper lip was larger than her lower lip, which is not the way she usually looks. Her lower lip appeared to have more fullness than she usually has naturally. Gwen is a total pro when it comes to doing her make-up, so maybe she just found some wild new lip lining trick to give her a bigger pout instead of hitting a doctor’s office.

    The singer seems to never have a moment where she isn’t stylish and glam. While heading into a recording session, she was totally camera ready with full makeup, including grey smokey eyes, peach cheeks and a nude lip color. She pulled her long platinum locks into a high ponytail and has dipped the ends in a dark brunette color. Even her outfit was so gorgeous for just a music making day. Gwen rocked a yellow, red and purple plaid shirt buttoned up all the way. She had a matching pleated miniskirt and used thigh high black boots to cover up her legs.

    Either way we’re sure boyfriend Blake Shelton, 42, will be happy to kiss her lips no matter what size they are. The couple is coming up on their three-year anniversary of dating in October and are more in love than ever. The pair fell in love after each got divorces over the summer of 2015. At the time they were both judges on NBC’s The Voice and leaned on each other for comfort and support. Soon that friendship blossomed into something more and by Nov. 3. 2015 they were publicly holding hands at a CMA’s after party.

    While some couples rush to tie the knot, even after three years together Blake and Gwen still haven’t made it to the altar. As we told you EXCLUSIVELY, neither of them are in a hurry to be married again. “There is no big secret as to why Blake and Gwen are not yet engaged,” a source close to Blake EXCLUSIVELY tells HollywoodLife.com, “it very simple — they’re just not in a rush to get that piece of paper. Neither of them need it to feel committed to each other.”

    Our insider added,  “They’re both undeniably ‘all in’ when it comes to their relationship. But, when it comes to getting the courts involved and dealing with all the paperwork they’d need to do as far as pre-nups, it gets overwhelming and frankly, not very romantic.
