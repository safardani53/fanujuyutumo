    LIVE with Kelly and Mark star Kelly Ripa has shown off a glam new look while filming her side project this week.

    Her makeup artist and hair stylist stood on either side of her and grinned for the camera as Kelly took center stage.

    When makeup artist Kristofer Buckle shared the snap on his own Instagram page, people were quick to comment.
