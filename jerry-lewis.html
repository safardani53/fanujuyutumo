    Jerry Lewis Early Life

    Jerry Lewis was born on March 16, 1926, in the musical family of Joseph Levitch in Newark, New Jersey, the United States to Daniel Levitch and Rachel ("Rae") Levitch (née Brodsky). He is an American and is of Russian Jewish descent.
