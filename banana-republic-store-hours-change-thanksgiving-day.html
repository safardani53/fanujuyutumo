    OTHER HOLIDAY OPENINGS

    Many retailers are shuttering their doors for the holiday to give workers a break to spend time with family.

    Some states like Maine even have "blue laws" that require some large stores to close on Thanksgiving, Christmas and Easter.

    Banana Republic's sister store, Old Navy, will also have some locations open for the day.

    Meanwhile, most Gap stores will not be open during the day but in the evening instead.

    The clothing retailer has its hours listed from 6 pm to 1 am on Thanksgiving, giving customers a chance to get started on their Black Friday shopping early.

    TJ Maxx, which operates its namesake brand, Marshalls, HomeGoods, Sierra, and Homesense, will be closed the entire day on Thanksgiving.

    Walmart said all stores will be closed for the fourth consecutive year.

    Best Buy has confirmed that it will be closed on Thanksgiving.

    And Target is following suit.

    Shoppers need not fret though.

    Online stores are always open and Cyber Monday deals await.
