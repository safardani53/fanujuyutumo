    BILL Burr is an in-demand comedian and actor known for his comedy specials You People Are All the Same and Bill Burr: Live at Red Rocks.

    Behind closed doors, he enjoys spending time with his wife, Nia Renee Hill — here's what we know about her.

    Who is Bill Burr's wife, Nia Renee Hill?

    Nia Renee Hill is an actress, writer, producer, and director born on June 2, 1978.

    Nia is widely known for her work in Lila, Long Distance and Did You Look For Work This Week?

    In 2017, she starred in Netflix's Santa Clarita Diet.

    Nia also worked as a voice actress for F Is for Family between 2017 and 2021.

    Additionally, the actress has built a following on her Instagram page, @niasalterego, with over 200,000 followers.

    How long have Bill Burr and Nia Renee Hill been married?

    After dating for many years, the couple officially tied the knot in October 2013.

    Bill has often referenced his wife and their marriage in his stand-up performances.

    For their 10th anniversary in October 2023, Nia took to Instagram to share a sweet compilation of their love through the years.

    The post was captioned: "20 years together. 10 years married, 2 children, 1 beautiful life. Happy Anniversary @wilfredburr #couplegoals #forever #family"

    Do Bill Burr and Nia Renee Hill have children?

    The couple share two children.

    Lola was born in January 2017 and their son arrived in the summer of 2020 - although they haven't revealed his name.

    In June 2020, Nia shared a sweet selfie cradling her newborn baby, paired with the caption: "Mama’s here. Always."

    Speaking to Jimmy Kimmel about his wife and kids, Bill said: "She gave me a beautiful baby boy, so I got a daughter and a son now, and it's awesome.

    "And then, fortunately, he emotionally seems to be wired like my wife.. so he's like totally chill. He just kind of cries when he's hungry."
