    “This is too much. I’m going to have a nervous breakdown,” she told InTouch in a tell-all interview. “Blake and Miranda are letting me take all the bullets. It’s wrong.” Cady was linked to this situation on July 22, just two days after the divorce news broke, when she went on a cryptic Twitter rant about lost love.

    Blake and Cady met via Twitter in 2011, and just three months after he married Miranda, he starred in his much-younger pal’s music video for “This Little Girl,” which was written after she went through a devastating breakup. But over the years, the pair allegedly continued to drunkenly tweet back-and-forth, and rumors that Blake had cheated with the Oklahoma native surfaced on more than one occasion.

    So when Cady started tweeting messages like, “I never quit loving you—you just ran” and “Its sick that I experienced a once in a lifetime love-still as strong as the day it happened- & have been punished every single day since,” she automatically began receiving backlash from fans who speculated she was referring to Blake.

    And in her tell-all, Cady admits, for the first time, that the 39-year-old is the man in her messages. “I tweeted because it was the only way for Blake to hear me,” she admitted. “I want to talk to him. If I could only be face-to-face with him, I have four questions for him. But he isn’t talking to me.”

    Blake and Miranda both kept completely tight-lipped when Cady was linked to the break up. They did have a cute exchange on Twitter that mocked all the divorce rumors, but only referenced reports that Miranda was pregnant, and made no mention of any cheating claims.
