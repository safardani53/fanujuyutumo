    07 Jun 2024, 00:00

    IRAN face Oman as the Asian Cup knockout stages get underway in the UAE.

    The Iranians were unbeaten in the group stages and topped Group D, whilst Oman squeezed through after an impressive 3-1 win over Turkmenistan in the final fixture.

    What time is kick-off?

    THIS final group game is all set for Sunday, January 20.
