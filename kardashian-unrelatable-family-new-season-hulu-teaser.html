    KARDASHIAN fans have criticized the famous family's new trailer for their reality show for being 'boring.'

    Hulu posted the reality show's trailer for season three of The Kardashians - which begins May 23.

    Kim kicked off the trailer and said: "Listen, everyone has their own truth of how they think something happened."

    Khloe then promised: "I wish I could tell you this is gonna be a mild, relaxing, serene season - it's not."

    Kourtney warned: "There's no sense of loyalty."

    After all that, Kim can be heard yelling: "You think I need your permission?"

    Kourtney's ex-husband Scott Disick, a family member fans are excited to see on the show, said: "I've never seen this much drama in my life."

    Soon after, the Jenners find their voice. Kendall said: "It all comes back to protecting my peace."

    Kylie actually touched on an important message: "We have huge influence. What are we doing with our power?"

    Finally, Kim finished off the trailer by saying: "So, let's talk about it."

    In between all the quotes and confessional messages, short but powerful clips of the new season were shared.

    Many of the sisters can be seen crying, laughing, dancing, and yelling. The only question is, what's the context?

    B-B-BORING

    Despite what the sisters said, fans saw the trailer on Twitter and immediately slammed it for being boring.

    One user re-tweeted the trailer and said: "Yeah, so whole lotta nothing going on with this trailer."

    Other KUWTK fans replied and said: "Couldn’t agree more."

    Another commented: "Same as the last three seasons 'so much drama' but the season is a snoozefest."

    A third added: "I can’t watch them anymore. Too boring and all it’s about is them getting glammed up every single day lol It’s all about how they look more than ever now."

    Someone else thought: "They have too much control over the series. It’s not dynamic in the same way early KUWTK was."

    TRADEMARK EVERYTHING

    Kylie recently filed a new trademark for the name of her fan-favorite lip product, Gloss Drip, The U.S. Sun can exclusively reveal.

    After filing trademarks for seven other Kylie Cosmetics products, it appears she is looking to expand her $1 billion business empire.

    She first filed the trademark for the name of her product Gloss Drip on March 13.

    Gloss Drip is joining the beauty mogul's seven other Kylie Cosmetics products, including Kylie Lip Kit by Kylie Jenner, Kybrow, Kyliner, Kyshadow, Kylighter, Kylash, and Glitter Eyes.

    The mother of two tends to brand everything off of her first name, although the Glitter Eyes and now Gloss Drip products appear to be an anomaly.

    The makeup mogul is fighting to claim ownership of the name, as Kylie Cosmetics competes with other beauty companies on the market.

    She already has her eyes set on expanding her reach in the beauty industry just a week after launching her own mascara.
