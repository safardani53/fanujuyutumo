    SEASON two of Outer Banks has dropped on Netflix on Friday, July 30, 2021.

    With new villains being introduced and while Rafe Cameron (Drew Starkey) is still on the loose, viewers witnessed a few on-screen deaths.

    Who dies in Outer Banks season 2?

    Some viewers may feel confused as this list does not include Sarah, JJ or Ward Cameron.

    Although JJ (Rudy Pankow) gets dragged in many dangerous situations, he finishes the second season alive, even after falling off a boat unconscious and injured.

    Fans had speculated could die after spotting a clue in the second season's trailer.

    Shot by her brother Rafe at the end of the second episode, Sarah (Madelyn Cline) is pronounced dead for a number of minutes after being brought to a doctor in Nassau.

    Sarah loses consciousness and eventually flatlines but after an intense few minutes, she wakes back up as John B. gives her CPR in an attempt to save her life.

    Charles Esten's Ward Cameron also narrowly escapes death in a shocking scene. After a warrant is issued for his arrest, he is found on a boat by the police of Tannyhill.

    While he admits to all of his crimes in the sixth episode, he manages to disappear into the boat, blowing it up in front of everyone on dock, including Sarah.

    In the ninth episode of Outer Banks season two, it is revealed that he faked his death in order to avoid arrest and protect his family with a timed explosion which left him the opportunity to jump off the boat with his scuba gear before it blew up.

    Unfortunately, the following characters were shown no such luck in the second season and are not expected to make their return to the show.

    Gavin

    Portrayed by Adam Vernier, Gavin's demise begins in the first episode of season two.

    When the Pogues track down the pilot of Ward's plane, which took off with the gold at the end of the first season, Gavin contacts him and tries to blackmail him.

    Upon seeing that Gavin has the gun that Rafe used to kill Sheriff Peterkin (Adina Porter) in season one, Ward beats him up in order to take it from him.

    The fight turns sour as Ward shoots him point-blank in the process.

    Gavin's body is then discarded by Ward, with the help of Rafe.

    Both make sure no evidence is left at the crime scene.

    Renfield

    Jesse C. Boyd's character is killed after he brings the Cross of Santo Domingo in a warehouse with Rafe to Carla Limbrey, portrayed by Elizabeth Mitchell.

    Renfield is killed by his half-sister after he pushes her to the floor, attempting to take off with the treasure.

    Carla is not willing to let go of the cross as she is convinced it contains the garment of the Savior, a Christian relic said to be able to heal many illnesses.
