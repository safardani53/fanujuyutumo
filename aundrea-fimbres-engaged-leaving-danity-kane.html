    03 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Aundrea Fimbres has good news and bad news.

    The good news, personally? She’s engaged. The bad news, for fans of Danity Kane? She will therefore be leaving the group.

    “I can tell you that two dreams of mine [have] been to be a singer and be on stage like this and to have a family,” Fimbres  told the audience at a show in San Francisco last week. “Well, in a couple months, that’s all coming true. I’m about to get married and I have this beautiful new little girl with me.”

    She then introduced her future step-daughter.

     

    The artist then explained what this will mean for her career.

    “I’ve gotten to do some amazing things with Danity Kane, but it’s time for me to start the next chapter in my life. So I thank you guys from the bottom of my heart…I love you so much.”

    Danity Kane, will continue to “kill it” in her absence, Aundrea assured the crowd.

    Remaining members in the group founded by Diddy on MTV’s Making the Band now include Shannon Bex, Aubrey O’Day and Dawn Richard.

    Danity Kane’s 13-date domestic tour resumed yesterday and continues through early June.
