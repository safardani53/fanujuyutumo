    DRIVING experience days are the perfect gift for the car lover or thrill seeker in your life - or even as a treat for yourself.

    They're not as expensive as you might think, either - many companies are now offering discounts on exciting track days and one-off experiences.

    If you’re looking for a great gift idea, or you want to get behind the wheel yourself, check out the best driving experience days you can buy in 2022 below.

    *If you click on a link in this article, we may earn affiliate revenue

    Are driving experience days safe?

    Absolutely - your safety is the utmost priority when it comes to driving experience days.

    If necessary, you will be kitted out with safety gear, and strapped in tight - all drivers are qualified race instructors too, and most driving experience days will give you a thorough safety briefing and chance to ask any questions before you begin.

    How long do driving experience days last?

    Driving experience days can last anything from 15 minutes up to four hours - but don't worry, no matter how brief it seems it will be an unforgettable experience.

    The length of each driving experience will be detailed in full on its associated website.

    If you’re buying for a younger audience, for £109 you can snap up a 60 minute off-roading experience - guarantee to make you hold your breath and get your heart racing.

    Designed for ages 10 and above, this experience lets the junior driver and thrill-seeker traverse a number of crazy off-road terrains and obstacles - and is an incredibly useful learning experience.

    Drivers get the chance to bomb around an off-road course in 17 tonnes of armoured vehicle on a former WWII bombing range - where war heroes themselves once walked.

    On your first lap, your instructor will demonstrate how to get going in the behemoth - then it's up to you to get your tank around the course.

    Trust us, this isn't a drive you'll forget easy.

    For kids and adults alike, get the once-in-a-lifetime experience to drive the Batmobile - a Lincoln Futura.

    For £99 you can buckle up in Buyagift’s Triple Supercar Driving Blast with free high speed passenger ride.

    That gets you a three mile drive in three supercars on the day with the choice between a Ferrari, Aston Martin, Porsche, Chevrolet and more.

    Also included in the affordable price of £99, reduced from £177, is a high speed passenger lap with a qualified race instructor to get the adrenaline flowing.

    The experience is available at 69 spots across the nation, so there's bound to be a rip-roaring race day near you.

    • Triple Supercar Driving Blast, £99 - buy here

    As well as having locations across the country to choose from, drivers can also race around Dunsfold airfield, AKA: the 'Home of Top Gear'.

    There's a full safety briefing and introduction by race qualified instructors before any experience day, too.

    That gets you 50 laps at over 30 locations across the UK including London, Manchester, Liverpool and Cardiff.

    Well, this is a little something different to talk about in the office kitchen. How about making like a Transformer and driving Optimus Prime for the day?

    Do you need a driving licence for a driving experience?

    Unless it's a Junior Drive Experience day, you'll need a full and clean driving licence.

    Bring your licence with you on the day, if you forget it you might not be able to take part!

    Don't worry, though, if you're buying a gift for a learner driver you can purchase a gift voucher to be redeemed when they pass.

    How old do you have to be to drive a supercar in the UK?

    If you're after a supercar driving experience, you're free to hop in a Ferrari or Lamborghini from the age of 17 as long as you have your licence.

    There is no legal difference when it comes to supercars, as long as you have your licence you're good to go.
