    WHEN Super Bowl Sunday arrives, there are a lot of things to look forward to, from the munchies to the halftime performance, and the game itself, but there are also the iconic commercials. 

    The new Booking.com commercial has three familiar faces that aim to attract viewers.

    Who stars in the Booking.com Super Bowl commercial?

    The 2022 Super Bowl commercial for Booking.com features actor Idris Elba seeking assistance from two men who have starred in their own commercials: Jonathan Goldsmith and Isaiah Mustafa.

    The British actor, who is known for his portrayals of Heimdall in the Thor and Avengers films and his roles in Pacific Rim, Beasts of No Nation, Molly’s Game, The Suicide Squad, and many more, calls up “two icons of the advertising world” for some advice on how to be a spokesman.

    Of course, because this is not Elba's first commercial job, he may not require as much guidance. 

    In 2019, the star of The Wire and Luther began working as a Ford spokesman, promoting the automaker's Mustang-inspired SUV. 

    In terms of Super Bowl’s star-studded commercials, Elba will be in great company as Seth Rogen and Paul Rudd advertise Lay's, Megan Thee Stallion and Charlie Puth promote Cheetos and Doritos, among the other stars lined up for gameday advertising.

    Who is Jonathan Goldsmith?

    Known well for being The Most Interesting Man in The World in his Dos Equis commercials from 2006-2016, Goldsmith is an American character actor who was born on September 26, 1938. 

    He began his acting career on the New York stage before moving on to cinema and television. 

    Goldsmith played a villain who was shot in the eyes by hero John Wayne in the 1976 film The Shootist.

    He also made guest appearances on series such as Gunsmoke, Bonanza, The Rockford Files, Hawaii Five-O, MacGyver, Charlie's Angels, Dynasty, T.J. Hooker, Knots Landing, and The A-Team.

    Dallas was his longest-running television series, with which he appeared 17 times.

    Who is Isaiah Mustafa?

    Born February 11, 1974, Mustafa is an American actor and former wide receiver in the National Football League (NFL).

    Mustafa is most recognized for his role as the lead character in the Old Spice advertisements The Man Your Man Could Smell Like. 

    He also played Luke Garroway on Freeform's fantasy series Shadowhunters and adult Mike Hanlon in It Chapter Two.

    Mustafa and his former wife have a daughter, Haley Mustafa. 

    He previously dated Catherine Joy (CJ) Perry, better known by her ring name Lana, a professional wrestler and actress. 

    On May 26, 2018, he married Lisa Mitchell after two years of dating.
