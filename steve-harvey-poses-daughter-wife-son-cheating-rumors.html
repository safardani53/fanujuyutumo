    FAMILY Feud host Steve Harvey has posed with his family at a recent event.

    They were all together despite rumors of marital discord.

    On Monday, Steve, 66, attended The Grio Awards with his rarely-seen family.

    According to the website, The Grios "honor icons, leaders and legends who have impacted the Black community and culture."

    Steve, at first, posed by himself.

    He wore a sparkly black suit jacket with a black button-down shirt underneath.

    Steve added black dress pants and shoes to the look, along with sepia-colored sunglasses.

    He looked away from the camera with a serious expression on his face.

    A gold curtain was behind him and a marbled floor was under his feet.

    Steve wore his wedding ring in the photos.

    STEVE'S FAMILY

    Steve simply captioned his Instagram post: "The Grio Awards."

    On the second slide, he posed with his wife, Marjorie, 59, and two of their children, Lori, 26, and Wynton, 26.

    The video featured the family standing in front of the gold curtain as photographers snapped pictures of them.

    Marjorie wore a long-sleeve white dress, with a black tie, black stockings, and black pointy-toed heels.

    Lori is a model, entrepreneur, and socialite and the adoptive daughter of Steve.

    Her biological father is not known to the public.

    She wore a black and white pleated suit, along with a white button-down shirt and black tie.

    Lori finished the look off with black shoes.

    Wynton is Steve's biological son and is rarely seen in public.

    He wore a black suit with a blue shirt underneath that showed off his chest.

    Wynton also paired the outfit with black dress shoes, a black clutch, and sunglasses.

    The family all smiled for the cameras.

    Steve has seven children in total- four biological and three adopted.

    CHEATING RUMORS

    In August, Steve assured fans that his marriage was fine, despite rumors that his wife had an affair with his personal chef and bodyguard, William Freedman, aka Big Boom, the Economic Times reported.

    Steve addressed the rumors at Invest Fest 2023 in Atlanta.

    The Family Feud host shut down the gossip before the event.

    He told the crowd, as seen in a TikTok video shared by an attendee: "Before I get started, just let me say, I'm fine. Marjorie's fine."

    Steve shrugged and continued: "I don't know what y'all doing, but find something else to do 'cause we fine."

    "Lord have mercy," he ended.

    Hours after that, Marjorie slammed the accusations on her Instagram account.

    She uploaded a screenshot of a website entitled "How to handle being lied about," which contained a quote from the Bible about “hurled insults” [seen below].

    In the caption, Marjorie addressed her relationship with her husband.

    “My husband and I don’t usually stop to address all the foolishness and lies that have been spread about us,” she wrote.

    “However to whom much is given much is required.

    "I understand that with my platform comes some sort of responsibility to those that may not be as strong as we are.
