    Brandy Clark was born on , in Morton, Washington. Talking about her nationality, she is an American and is of white ethnicity. As a child, she was highly influenced by the country-pop and traditional country music artists such as Loretta Lynn, Merle Haggard, Ronnie Milsap, and Barbara Mandrell.

    Clark picked up the guitar for the first time when she was nine. During that time she also used to sing in school musicals. When her mother noticed her talent, she encouraged Clark to write songs. However, she paused her musical career for some time while in high school, and rather allocated her energies to sports. Later, she attended college on a basketball scholarship at Central Washington University.

    However, she quit sports and moved back to her home where she joined community college. At the time she joined a band along with her mother and a friend and also enrolled at Belmont University and studied commercial music.

    Upon graduation, she landed up working with Leadership Music. In Chart pursued her career in music professionally and came out as a songwriter for the song “Mama’s Broken Heart”, which eventually ranked no. 2 on Billboard Hot Country Songs and on Country Airplay charts.

    Throughout her career, Clark songs have been recorded by Kacey Musgraves, Sheryl Crow, LeAnn Rimes, Billy Currington, Miranda Lambert, Reba McEntire, The Band Perry, and Darius Rucker.

    In addition to her music career, Clark has released 2 albums “12 Stories” and “Big Day in a Small Town”. Recently she has released the live album from Los Angeles.

    Though Brandy Clark has been nominated for several awards like “Grammy Awards”, “American Country Awards”, “Country Music Association” while she is the winner of two prestigious awards “CMA Awards” for the category song of the year-“Follow Your Arrow” and “British Country Music Association Awards” for International Song of the year, “Stripes”.

    But when it comes to her personal life, Clark is quite confidential. Although many sources identify her as a lesbian, the claims are not yet proved to be true. As per our record, Clark is currently single and unmarried.
