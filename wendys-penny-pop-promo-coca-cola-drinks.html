    IT'S the happiest time of year at Wendy's with the restaurant offering a brand new promo fans can enjoy for a penny.

    Starting December 13, customers can fill their cups with Coca-Cola products for only one cent by using the Wendy's app.

    No additional purchase is required to take part in the promotion, according to a press release.

    "Enjoy the sips of the season with festive flavors, like Dave's Cherry Cream Soda or Sprite Vanilla, for a dash of holiday spirit in your cup," read the release.

    There are over 100 drinks to choose from as part of the Penny Pops promotion.

    All users have to do is open the Wendy's app and find a gift a small one-cent Coca-Cola drink each day.
