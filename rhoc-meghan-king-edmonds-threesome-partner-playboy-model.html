    REAL Housewives of Orange County's Meghan King Edmonds and her ex Jim's threesome partner was Playboy model Kortnie O'Connor, it's been claimed.

    The reality TV star recently claimed her estranged husband, 49, ran off with a woman they once had a threesome with on her podcast Intimate Knowledge.

    And now Page Six have reported that multiple sources have confirmed that Kortnie, 35, is in fact the woman dating Jim.

    According IMDB, she was once Playboy's "Cyber Girl of the Month" in 2007 and also featured in music videos for Nickelback as well as Saliva and Hinder.

    The California native, who mainly modelled lingerie and swimwear, previously defended her choice to pose nude, telling The Nasty Show that Playboy is "not porn."

    In a 2009 Rukus magazine interview, Kortnie was asked what she looked for in a man.

    She said: "Nice skin, athletic and someone that is well educated on life. I need someone with a good sense of humor also" and added that she was attracted to "successful men and good style."

    The former Playboy model has moved on from life infront of the camera and now works as a luxury property manager.

    The revalation comes after Meghan claimed Jim ran off with a woman they once had a threesome with before the former MLB star accused her of spreading lies for publicity.

    Speaking on her on her Intimate Knowledge podcast, the Bravo star told her co-hosts Brooke Burke and Lila Darville: "We had just gotten married.

    "I knew Jim had this bad boy past and we were newlyweds and trying to have fun.

    "He wanted to have a threesome… We decided to have a threesome - a consensual adult decision - with a friend of mine.

    “I felt very comfortable with her. It just felt like, ‘OK, if I’m going to do this, this can be with who, and I’m comfortable with that.’”

    However, she also revealed she didn't feel comfortable telling other people about the experience because she wasn't proud of it.

    When Meghan’s co-host asked if she believes he and the friend had an affair, she responded: “I think they probably had sex more than one time.”

    Meghan then revealed that Jim, 49, took his new girlfriend to Cabo and that she allegedly used his credit card to purchase items for the trip.

    She said: "They are in Cabo right now. This girl who I thought was my friend, who I cast aside my [uncomfortableness] with a threesome, everything that I had in my head about a threesome being weird and gross because I was fun and wanted to do things for my husband.

    "And then this girl betrayed me."

    Following the podcast, Jim responded to Meghan's claims by accusing her of having affairs throughout their marriage.

    He told E! News: "I am tired of the lies for publicity. I filed for divorce over 90 days ago. Meghan has filed as well. The marriage is and has been over."

    "I was invited to a surprise birthday party this weekend for one of my best friends and decided to bring a date. There is no affair going on here."

    Jim recently took down all pictures of Meghan from his Instagram to make the divorce official.

    The baseball broadcaster also said the woman is not Meghan's friend, as they haven't spoken in over three years.

    He continued: "As far as threesomes go, there were a more than few initiated by Meghan and only Meghan. Recently, I have learned that she carried on with a few of these women without me being present.

    "Would that not be considered cheating? How can she have a podcast talking about intimacy and relationships when she has neither?"
