    A MAN has admitted he's left Cadbury "fuming" after complaining that his box of Roses was missing any orange cremes.

    Charlie Murphy has been sharing numerous videos comparing Christmas chocolates, and counting the flavours in each one.

    But in one of the clips, he revealed his anger after noticing that there were none of his favourite tangy orange chocolates in the tub.

    The video caught Cadbury's attention, and prompted a reply from the chocolate company.

    "Cadbury's are fuming with me. Absolutely fuming," he said in another TikTok.

    "And here's why.
