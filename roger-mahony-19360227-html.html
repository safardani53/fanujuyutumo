    Roger Michael Cardinal Mahony KGCHS is an American cardinal and retired bishop of the Roman Catholic Church, serving as Archbishop of Los Angeles from 1985 to 2011. Prior to being appointed Bishop General of Los Angeles, he served as Bishop Auxiliary of Fresno from 1975 to 1980 and as Bishop of Stockton from 1980 to 1985. Mahony was instrumental in covering up the sexual abuse of many young people in the Church. Born in the Hollywood neighborhood of Los Angeles and raised in the San Fernando Valley area, Mahony was ordained a priest in the Diocese of Monterey-Fresno in 1962. He was appointed Auxiliary Bishop of the Diocese of Fresno in January 1975 and consecrated bishop in March 1975. After that, he was appointed Bishop of Stockton in 1980. In 1985, Mahony was Pope John Paul II appointed Archbishop of Los Angeles, and became the first person from Los Angeles to hold office.
