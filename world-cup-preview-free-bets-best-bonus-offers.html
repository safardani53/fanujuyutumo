    That'll be a quiet night at the library… not!

    Ghana have revenge firmly in their sights when they take on Uruguay at Qatar's Al Janoub Stadium.

    A draw would actually suffice on Friday, providing South Korea do not beat Portugal by two goals or more.

    Uruguay's path to the last 16 is to defeat Ghana in their final match and hope that South Korea fail to beat Portugal.
