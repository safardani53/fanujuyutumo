    Jackie Goldschneider accused Jennifer Aydin of spying on her husband Evan, and their relationship later soured. A heated argument between the two broke their romance in Season 12. Since the pool party brawl with Margaret Josephs, the RHONJ star has been a staunch supporter of Aydin. Jackie said she understood Jennifer’s anguish at her husband’s affair being made public and wanted to be there for her as a friend. However, the dynamics have shifted with the dinner party in episode five. After Melissa’s violent altercation with Jennifer, Jackie opted to stand with Melissa and left the party with her and Margaret Josephs, dissatisfied with Aydin for starting the incident.

    Jackie Goldschneider and Jennifer Aydin of RHONJ got into a fight.

    Melissa accused her of snooping on Jackie’s husband, Evan’s infidelity at Jennifer’s dinner party. The Goldscheiders supported Aydin, but the drama caused them to become separated.

    Jackie confessed in a confessional:

    “If she had discovered Evan had cheated on her, she would have yelled it for everyone to hear. Of course, [she] was attempting to harm me, just as Teresa was. Be a true lady and acknowledge it.”

    After Melissa and Jennifer’s violent argument on RHONJ, Jackie agreed with Melissa and stated she was “done” with Aydin. She confessed in a confessional:

    “I can’t believe I ever supported Jennifer in the first place. Jennifer sinks her heels into my hubby with no remorse. She grabs one of my pals’ hands. Jennifer has gotten to me.”

    The RHONJ star During Teresa’s pool party, Jackie made a remark about Bill that irritated Jennifer. She admitted to Evan that the statement was made with no malice against Aydin but as a show of solidarity.

    “When I made the remark, it was in the context of… Jennifer made me sad. She accepted full responsibility for Bill’s pain…and for that, she took all of the friendship I given her and threw it back in my face…I’m done with her.”

    When Dolores Catania questioned Jackie about how she switched sides, she replied:

    “As a mother, I wanted to be there for her since she was going through a difficult time. And then you went and informed her [about the remark] for what purpose? to start a fight? That was misunderstood by you. I said that to support her. I felt terrible for her until last night, when she went all-in on everything and completely wrecked everything.”

    Jennifer now has just Teresa by her side while her bond with Jackie frays. In the forthcoming episode’s promo, the househusbands questioned Bill about his wife’s behavior, but he walked away.
