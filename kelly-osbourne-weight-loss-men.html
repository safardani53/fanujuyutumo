    KELLY Osbourne displayed her 85lb weight loss during a shopping trip with a friend in Los Angeles.

    The 36-year-old - who has lost six stone since her gastric sleeve in 2018 - looked casual in boyfriend jeans as she strolled around The Grove.

    Kelly finished off her look with a black satin bomber jacket and black Converse sneakers.

    She wore her pink-hued hair in space buns and added a protective black face mask.

    Laden down with shopping bags, the former Fashion Police judge and her pal stopped off for lunch at an alfresco restaurant.

    Kelly's outing comes after she proudly revealed she turned down seven men who wanted to date her after she lost weight.

    She told her mom Sharon Osbourne on The Talk that she'd been interested in each of the men at one point.

    "Do you know what it was? It was all guys who I had shown interest in before [who wanted to date me]," she said.

    "But [they] had all made comments like, 'She's great, but she's too fat'

    "So it was all of those people who came back around again, so I was like 'Nah nah.'"

    Kelly wouldn't go into detail about her current dating life, telling her mom: "Let's just say I'm having fun."

    However she has been pictured on a number of dates with 21-year-old TikTok star Griffin Johnson.

    Most recently the pair were seen enjoying dinner together in West Hollywood.

    Ozzy Osbourne's daughter lost a significant amount of weight after having a gastric sleeve surgery two years ago.

    "I had surgery. I don't give a f**k what anyone has to say," Kelly declared. "I did it, I'm proud of it, they can suck s**t. I did the gastric sleeve.

    "All it does is change the shape of your stomach. I got that almost two years ago.

    "I will never ever ever lie about it ever. It is the best thing I have ever done."

    The outspoken TV personality celebrated turning 36 last month with a penis cake.
