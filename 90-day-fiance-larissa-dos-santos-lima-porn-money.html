    90 DAY Fiance star Larissa Dos Santos Lima teased fans about a possible porn career.

    The Brazilian "queen," as she calls herself, had also told fans she would be creating an OnlyFans page.

    Larissa, 34, talked to her fans on Instagram Live about what it would take for her to start a porn career.

    According to The Blast, she told her viewers: "People are posting in the press that I’m going to be a porn star.

    "No, I’m not going to be a porn star. Half a million dollars. $500,000, I would do."

    However, she assured her fans that becoming a porn star was not on the agenda for her.

    She explained: "I came [on Instagram Live] because I got a lot of questions, this and that, people posting that I was going to be a porn star.

    "Driving me nuts. It’s not true."

    The Brazilian beauty had previously told fans she was thinking of starting an OnlyFans page at some point in the future.

    Back in May, she posted a plain-and-simple screengrab from Instagram.

    A fan had incessantly asked her if she was going to create an OnlyFans account, but she didn't answer him directly.

    Instead, she shared a messages with big red letters that read: "SOON."

    Aside from confirming her plans, Larissa revealed in the same Live that she wasn't sure what type of content she would upload on the account.

    The 33-year-old had also confirmed previously she was returning to the TLC show despite getting a divorce.

    Along with a picture of her in front of a filming camera, she wrote: "As many of you already know, I'll be on the next season of 90 Day Fiance Happily Ever After.

    "The season premiere is Sunday, June 14 at 8 p.m. ET/PT."

    She continued her post: "Be sure to watch and find out what is going on with my immigration, court, and love life!!

    "I can't talk about these things now, but soon you will find out all sorts of things about my life.
    "I wish to extend a special Thank You to all the 90 Day Fiance crew and producers that work so hard.

    "It was the best to be filming with you all again!"

    Back in December 2019, RadarOnline.com claimed she wrapped up her criminal assault case.

    Court records showed she completed the court-ordered six months of domestic violence classes, paid off her fines and completed 48 hours of community service.

    She was originally accused of domestic violence after Colt claimed she attacked him and faked her own injuries during a fight.

    Las Vegas police were able to confirm that her wounds were self-inflicted and she was charged with first-degree domestic battery in January 2019.
