    10 May 2024, 00:00

    ANA de Armas chopped off her long hair and debuted a new cut as her split from boyfriend Ben Affleck continues to heat up.

    The famous pair seemed to drift apart after moving in together.

    The actress made an appearance on a YouTube stream as she took a couple of minutes to share some kind words for her close pal Claudia Muma.

    The Knives Out star, 32, showed off her new bob and sported a white tee as she also wore some jewelry.

    The star's new look comes just a short time after calling it quits with the A-lister - a source told People recently: "Ben is no longer dating Ana."

    The insider told the outlet: "She broke it off. Their relationship was complicated. Ana doesn't want to be Los Angeles based and Ben obviously has to since his kids live in Los Angeles."

    Another source chimed in: "This is something that was mutual and something that is completely amicable.

    "They are in different points in their lives and there is deep love and respect there. Ben continues to want to work on himself. He has three jobs lined up and he’s a solid father at home.

    "They are both happy with where they are in their lives."

    Things seemed to hit a snag once the couple found themselves under the same roof as Ana was living in Ben's Los Angeles mansion.

    Another source previously told In Touch that the "honeymoon phase" was done once they shared a living space.

    The insider said: “Ana rocketed to stardom since dating Ben and has a bunch of movies lined up.

    “She’s young, ambitious and her career will always come first.

    “While Ben wants her to succeed, he’s needy and feels lost and lonely when she’s off gallivanting the world without him.”

    It was just two months ago that Ana and Ben, who began their relationship in early 2020, were spotted during a heavy PDA session on the set of their film Deep Water.

    The outing came after fans wondered if they had broken up as they hadn't been seen together in three months - however, now it looks like they have indeed gone their separate ways.

    Ana married actor and model Marc Clotet in 2011, but they ended things in 2013.

    Ben, 48, was married to actress Jennifer Garner for 10 years before they said goodbye in 2015.

    The former couple welcomed three kids during their relationship: daughters Violet, 14, and Seraphina, 11 and son Samuel, eight.
