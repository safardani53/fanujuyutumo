    The former NFL player and a future Panthers’ Hall Of Honor inductee, Julius Peppers’s yet-to-be wife, is Claudia Sampedro. Despite all the speculations, they haven’t tied the knot.

    The Cuban-born Sampedro first came to attention when people started comparing her to Kim Kardashian. Today the Kim lookalike is an aspiring entrepreneur. 

    Julius and Claudia are parents to three beautiful children.

    The former defensive and outside linebacker in the NFL, Julius Frazier Peppers, was born on January 18, 1980, in Wilison, North Carolina, U.S.

    He played collegiate football for the North Carolina Tar Heels, where he finished his career ranked second all-time in Tar Heels history with 30.5 sacks.

    Moreover, during Pepper’s time at Southern Nash HS, he majored in Afro-American studies.

    The NFL All-Decade Team (2000s) athlete played for three teams during his professional career, Carolina Panthers, Chicago Bears, and Green Bay Packers.

    He started his career with Carolina Panthers, but after 8 years with the Panthers, Chicago Bears signed him on a six-year deal worth $91.5 million. 

    Julius was signed by Green Bay Packers in 2014 on a contract worth $30 million.

    However, after nearly eight years, the Panthers legend returned for his second spell.

    He finished his career as the all-time leader in sacks in Panthers history. 

    Eventually, after 17 years of showcasing his greatness in the NFL, Peppers hung his boots on February 1, 2019.

    Julius Peppers Wife Claudia Sampedro Is An Entrepreneur

    Julius Peppers’s yet-to-be wife is Claudia Sampedro. While Julius currently relishes his retired life, his partner Claudia is involved in e-commerce.

    Claudia got into the entrepreneurial world after she launched her clothing brand WPOISE back in 2017.

    However, she decided to close this company in early 2021.

    Despite this setback, she didn’t give up and founded On The Rise Collective, a company selling health and beauty products while also fostering people on how to earn 6 figures just from Wifi.

    Claudia is also the founder of Body By Claudia & Lynette, which is a course-selling business selling workout and meal plans for females.

    The aspiring entrepreneur is also an Associate Executive Director of Monat, a company selling skincare products.

    In addition to this, Claudia is a professional model who has been featured on the cover page of several magazines, including “Mixed Magazine” and “Face 2 Face Magazine”.

    Julius And Claudia’s Kids

    Julius and Claudia met through a mutual friend at a party and eventually started dating.

    The two of them are parents to three kids. 

    However, the situation regarding their children is a bit tangled.

    Claudia already had a daughter, Keena Skye, born in 2009, from her previous relationship.

    Likewise, Julius also had a son named Elijah Peppers, who was born to his former girlfriend Lia.

    Together, Julius and Claudia have a son, Amaresito, born on September 21, 2015.

    Surprisingly in an episode of WAGS Miami, Claudia asked if Julius at least thinks about getting married someday. 

    To this, Julius replied, “I think that, um, I don’t feel the pressure to get married at all or engaged, “I like the space that we’re in right now, like where we’re at. I don’t feel like we need to put any extra pressure or any expectations on it when it’s good right now.”
