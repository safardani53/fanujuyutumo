    No words can describe how good Toni Braxton looked last night at the Grammy Awards, but we’ll try. The legendary singer walked the red carpet liked she owned the place in a completely sheer dress that hugged her every curve. The dress was adorned with purple sparkles in a unique, art deco pattern that managed to cover just enough of her body to make the outfit legal to wear in public. Scroll down to see a full-length pic of Toni’s bedazzled gown.

    We simply refuse to believe this goddess is 51 years old! Not only was she totally one of the best dressed stars of the night, but her hair and makeup game was on point, too. Her long hair was parted down the middle and impossibly straight and smooth. She went for understated makeup and a nude lip, which offset her wild dress perfectly. Toni walked the red carpet together, so we have a feeling that her on-again finacé, Birdman, must have been totally jealous missing out!

    Toni and Birdman split shortly after getting engaged, but the couple made it work. Fans knew something was up when she posted on Instagram about “starting a new chapter” and shared some super sexy photos — Birdman not included. Just three weeks later, though, Birdman surprised her in concert and it was clearly back on. Good thing, too, considering Toni’s family is totally rooting for them!

    A source close to the “Un-Break My Heart” singer told HollywoodLife EXCLUSIVELY in January that, “Toni’s sisters and her mom all really like Birdman. None of them stepped in and told Toni she should take Birdman back, but they’re rooting for them. The Braxton family would love to see Toni and Birdman make it down the aisle. They were all sad when Toni called off the engagement.”
