    The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip (RHUGT) is jetting off for another vacation in season 4, and this time, the OG cast members of RHONY are making a comeback.

    If you’re experiencing the winter blues, the stars of The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip season 4 are heading to St Barts for fans to live vicariously. From skinny dipping and yacht, the six Bravo ladies are letting loose – and it wouldn’t be a vacay if Luann de Lesseps isn’t kept entertained by 10 handsome men.

    Cast of The Real Housewives Ultimate Girls Trip season 4: RHONY Legacy

    Kelly Killoren Bensimon

    Age: 55

    Job: real estate agent

    Partner: Scott Litner

    Children: 2

    Net Worth: $10 Million

    Kelly Killoren starred in RHONY seasons 2 to 4, giving fans some of the most outrageous one-liners in Housewives history. During the memorable season 3 Scary Island episode, Kelly went into a meltdown and didn’t hold back with the insults, calling Bethenny Frankel “creepy” and a “h**-bag”.

    Killoren has more than the Bravo show to look forward to; she is planning two weddings with fiance Scott Litner. The couple became engaged in July 2023 after a year of dating. She shares two daughters – Sea, 25, and Teddy, 23 – with ex-husband Gilles Bensimon.

    Luann de Lesseps

    Age: 58

    Job: TV star and model

    Partner: Single

    Children: 2

    Net Worth: $25 million

    Luann de Lesseps is a true Bravo veteran, having appeared in Real Housewives of New York since its premiere season up to series 13. Luann would’ve likely stayed on the show if the 2023 reboot did not recruit an all-new line-up.

    Fans have watched Lesseps’ marriage unfold over the past decade, including her divorce from Alexandre de Lesseps, and transition into the single life in the Big Apple. She is currently not seeing anyone so she will be mingling in St Barts.

    Dorinda Medley

    Age: 59

    Job: TV star

    Partner: Single

    Children: 1

    Net Worth: $10 million

    Dorinda Medley was promoted to main cast in season 7 after a guest appearance in series 4. Her exit after season 12 was not a mutual decision, claiming that “she did not have a good season this year,” so Bravo fired her.

    The 59-year-old explained that her father’s illness, the devastating flood in her Blue Stone Manor home, and her breakup with boyfriend John Mahdessian took a toll in season 12.

    Dorinda’s second husband, Ralph Lynch, died in 2011 and she went on to date Mahdessian, the owner of the high-end dry cleaner Madame Paulette, until 2019.

    Sonja Morgan

    Age: 60

    Job: Entrepreneur and philanthropist

    Partner: Single

    Children: 1

    Net Worth: $8 million

    Expect Sonja Morgan to get a little TMI on Girls Trip as she recently spilled details on her exciting romance with a “Viking”.

    Describing her new beau as having “a beard, a mustache, white a little gray,” the Bravo star joked about their physical relationship, saying: “The third time I was with him I was like, ‘Have you always had a beard?’. And I never swap spit. I don’t want anyone’s spit in my mouth.”

    We’re sure her 23-year-old daughter, Quincy Adams, was less than pleased to hear about her mom’s bedroom antics. Sonja shares Quincy with her ex-husband John Adams Morgan. They split in 2006 and the housewife went on to date Million Dollar Listing New York star Ryan Serhant.

    Ramona Singer

    Age: 67

    Job: Businesswoman and real estate agent

    Partner: William “Bill” Luby

    Children: 1

    Net Worth: $16 million

    Like Luann, Ramona Singer starred in the first 13 seasons of RHONY until the reboot and Girls Trip season 1. Outside of reality TV, Singer worked at Douglas Elliman but was axed from the job in early November reportedly due to her alleged use of a racial slur during a conversation with a RHONY season 13 crew member, writes Vanity Fair.

    Ramona denied the allegations but Bravo proceeded to cut her from November’s BravoCon. Nonetheless, she will be making a comeback in Ultimate Girls Trip.

    Kristen Taekman

    Age: 46

    Job: Model and entrepreneur

    Partner: Josh Taekman

    Children: 2

    Net Worth: $20 million

    Kristen Taekman entered the industry as a model in New York, Paris, Italy, and Australia. She dived into the business world with her successful brand of high-end greeting card boxes called 2nd Street Press.

    Today, she counts NYC’s TriBeCa as her home base with her husband of 10 years, Josh, and their two children: Cash, 6, and Kingsley, 3.

    When she’s not filming, you’ll find her updating her parenting and fashion blog, Last Night’s Look.
