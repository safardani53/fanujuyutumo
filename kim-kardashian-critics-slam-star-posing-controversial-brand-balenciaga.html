    Kardashian critics have slammed Kim for her new photos with a controversial luxury fashion brand.

    The Hulu star made many appearances at fashion events during Paris Fashion Week.

    On Sunday, Kim, 42, and several other A-list celebs attended the Balenciaga fashion show in France.

    On the company's Instagram page, Kim posed in one of the outfits in the Summer 2024 collection.

    In front of ruby red curtains, the mom of four didn't share a smile while wearing a skintight black dress while holding onto a sparkly heel in her hand.

    The Skims founder wore black tights underneath as well as a matching pair of shoes while her hair was damp and pushed behind her shoulders.

    BALENCIAGA BACKLASH

    The luxury fashion house faced the wrath of parents on social media after images of children cuddling unsettling soft toys were shared online.

    In November, Balenciaga shared photos that were originally meant to promote gifts - including reusable coffee cups and dog harnesses.

    However, critics slammed the campaign on social media due to the bear's strikingly bold BDSM attire.

    The stuffed animal had spiky leather bracelets around the bear's arms and legs.

    Along with other dubious leather features, the teddy appeared to don a lacy black thong.
