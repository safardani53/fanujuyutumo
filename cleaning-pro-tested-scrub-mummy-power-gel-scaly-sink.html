    A CLEANING pro has tested the brand new Scrub Mummy Power Gel to see how it holds up against common cleaning woes. 

    Kirsten took to TikTok to share her first impressions of the new universal household cleaner - and while it’s ideal for tackling grubby grout or scaly sinks, she found wasn’t a hit when it came to the hob. 

    Known online as @southernescape, the savvy mum from North Carolina, USA, regularly shares her top cleaning tips and handy home hacks with her 1.4 million followers. 

    In a new reel, she said: “Oh, what is this? Scrub Mummy Power Gel. 

    “Okay, we’re going to try a bunch of different stuff.”

    During the 31-second reel, Kristen is seen trialling the new product, first on her limescale-covered sink.

    As per the instructions, the cleaning fan is seen dunking her Scrub Daddy sponge into the white gel, before wiping it across her steel basin. 

    She said: “Okay, we’re going to start with the kitchen sink.

    “First impression, oh I love the bubbles. Chef’s kiss.”

    Hoping the impressive results continue, Kirsten goes on to try it on her hob, focusing on some dried grease.

    “Hmm… didn’t work. Okay, moving on.”

    Finally, she tests out whether it’ll leave her kitchen grout looking good as new. 

    She added: “My son squished a blackberry into my kitchen floor because he don’t give a f***.
